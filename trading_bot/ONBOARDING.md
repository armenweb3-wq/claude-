# ZENITH — Getting Started (send to friends)

Welcome to **ZENITH** — automated crypto trading that runs on **your own** Bybit
account. Your money never leaves your account; the key you connect **cannot
withdraw**. You stay in full control and can turn it off anytime.

> ⚠️ **Risk:** crypto trading with leverage is high-risk — you can lose some or
> all of the money you trade with. Results are not guaranteed. Only use money you
> can afford to lose. This is not financial advice.

It takes about **10 minutes**. Follow these steps in order.

---

## 1. Create your account
1. Open: **https://claude-1-vi6w.onrender.com/app**
2. Tap **"New here? Create an account"**
3. Enter a username, your email, and a password (8+ characters)
4. Tick the box to accept the terms → **Create account**

## 2. Get a Bybit account ready
- If you don't have one, sign up at **bybit.com** and complete verification.
- **Fund it** with the amount you want the bot to trade (start small — e.g. $50–100).

## 3. Set ISOLATED margin (important — 30 seconds)
This makes sure a single bad trade can never risk more than that one trade.
1. In Bybit, open any **Perpetual/Futures** market (e.g. BTCUSDT)
2. Tap the **Cross / Isolated** toggle (near the leverage button)
3. Choose **Isolated** → confirm

*(The bot also tries to set this for you automatically, but please set it too.)*

## 4. Create an API key (trade only — NO withdrawal)
1. Bybit → profile icon → **API** → **Create New Key** → **System-generated**
2. Name it `ZenithBot`
3. Permissions:
   - ✅ **Unified Trading** → **Orders** + **Positions** (Trade)
   - ❌ **Withdraw** → leave **OFF** (the app rejects keys that can withdraw)
4. IP restriction: leave as **No IP** for now
5. **Copy the API Key and Secret** (the secret shows only once)

## 5. Connect your key in the app
- Back in the app (Step 1 of 3 → Connect exchange) → paste the **API Key + Secret** → **Connect & verify**

## 6. Activate
- On the activation step, enter the **access code** the admin gave you → you're activated instantly (free).

## 7. Turn on Telegram alerts (recommended)
- Menu (☰) → **Profile** → **Connect Telegram** → press **Start** in Telegram
- You'll get every trade open/close + a daily summary, and a link to the community.

## 8. Start the bot
- On the dashboard, flip the **Automated trading** switch to **ON**.
- That's it — the bot now trades your account automatically.

---

## What to expect
- The bot waits for high-confidence setups; some days it makes no trades — that's normal.
- Each trade has a **stop-loss** and takes profit in steps (TP1/TP2/TP3).
- You can **close any trade** or **turn the bot off** anytime from the dashboard.
- Add the app to your **home screen** (Add to Home Screen) for an app-like experience.

Questions? Message the admin or the community channel. Trade safe. 🚀
