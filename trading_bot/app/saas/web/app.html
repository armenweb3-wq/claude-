Step 1 of 3 — Connect exchange

Link your Bybit account so the bot can trade for you. Your funds stay in your account — the key we use cannot withdraw. About 2 minutes.

1
Log in to Bybit → top-right profile → API.
2
Tap Create New Key → choose System-generated API Keys.
3
Name it ZenithBot. API key type: Unified Trading.
4
Permissions: enable Contract → Orders & Positions (Trade). Leave "Withdraw" OFF.
5
(Optional) restrict to no IP for now. Tap Submit.
6
Copy the API Key and Secret (secret shows once) and paste below.
We verify your key with Bybit and reject any key that can withdraw. Your key is encrypted at rest and only used to place trades on your own account.
